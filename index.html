About Dirt Divers Mining Co.

Dirt Divers Mining Co. has been consciously hand mining minerals and gemstones in Colorado for the better part of two decades. They do not incorporate any machinery or use any chemicals to clean their stones. Which mainly include Quartz & other varioations of it. They work with the BLM and USFS to keep the forrest, animals and vegetation safe for future generations to enjoy.
